1) Recommended formation: 4-2-3-1 for Arsenal. Crowds the central lanes against Chelsea's midfield three, frees a #10 to drift between the lines.

2) Pressing strategy: Trigger the press the moment a centre-back plays square to a full-back. Near-side #8 jumps to the ball, striker curves to cut the switch.

3) Key battle: Saka vs. Cucurella on Arsenal's right. Winning that 1v1 repeatedly tilts the whole right channel.
